(see [LICENSE](LICENSE)). Noncommercial use is free.

**Commercial use requires a paid license.** If you or your organization use
UltraCoS in or for a business, contact **mikko.parkkola@iki.fi** to arrange one.

## What a commercial license covers

- The right to use the UltraCoS codec commercially.
- Access to refreshed calibration dialects as model tokenizers change
  (see [calibration/](calibration/) and [METHODOLOGY.md](METHODOLOGY.md)).

## What is honestly on offer (and what is not)

UltraCoS does not claim a specific savings figure it has not measured. The codec
performs lossless, fail-open tool-result compaction; the value of that on your
workload is measurable on your own traffic and varies. We do not publish
savings percentages we cannot back with measurement.

For teams running Claude Code at scale, a separate offering — **spend visibility
and prompt-cache protection** (preventing cache-invalidation events that re-bill
large prefixes) — is available on inquiry. It is the higher-value layer and is
priced on the visibility and protection it provides, not on a compression
percentage.
